Chae Ahn is a graphic designer based in Seoul. She has completed multiple projects on typography, identity and web development. She prefers cohesive details rather than macroscopic ones. Likes to disassemble and reconstruct diagrams, images, and texts in graphic design. For any business inquires, feel free to reach me.
